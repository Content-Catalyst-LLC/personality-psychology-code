# Methods Notes

This scaffold models hierarchical trait structure through linked psychometric dimensions:

1. item-level responses
2. facet-level scores
3. broad domain scores
4. reliability by level
5. item-to-facet aggregation
6. facet-to-domain aggregation
7. domain-versus-facet prediction
8. bandwidth-fidelity comparison
9. within-domain profile dispersion
10. hierarchy consistency
11. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real psychometric study would require validated instruments, factor-analytic model checking, reliability evidence, construct-validity evidence, measurement-invariance testing, missing-data analysis, sampling review, fairness analysis, privacy safeguards, and appropriate ethical/legal review for any applied or consequential use.
