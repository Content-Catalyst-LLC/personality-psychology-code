# Upgrade Manifest

This directory has been upgraded into a professional research scaffold for the article **Personality, Creativity, and the Forms of Imagination**.

## Upgrade scope

The scaffold now includes:

- research-grade README;
- methods documentation;
- data dictionary and provenance notes;
- synthetic demonstration dataset;
- Python, R, Julia, SQL, Go, Rust, C, C++, and Fortran workflows;
- validation script;
- reproducibility and responsible-use documentation;
- structured outputs folders for generated figures and tables.

## Core research framing

The article treats creativity as a multi-component outcome shaped by personality traits, domain knowledge, persistence, social support, and domain context. The workflows separate divergent thinking, creative achievement, and everyday creativity rather than collapsing creativity into a single score.

## Use boundary

This scaffold is designed for research demonstration, teaching, transparent modeling, and reproducible analysis. It is not designed for clinical assessment, hiring, screening, diagnosis, ranking individuals, or workplace evaluation.
