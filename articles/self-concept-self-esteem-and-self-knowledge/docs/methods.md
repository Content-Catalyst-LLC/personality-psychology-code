# Methods Notes

This scaffold models self-concept, self-esteem, and self-knowledge through linked dimensions:

1. self-ratings across warmth, conscientiousness, emotional stability, and openness
2. synthetic observer ratings across the same domains
3. self–other gaps and self-knowledge accuracy
4. actual, ideal, and ought self-discrepancy
5. self-concept positivity
6. self-esteem
7. social recognition and external devaluation
8. wellbeing
9. self-system profiles and vulnerable self-system patterns

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, careful sampling, multi-informant design, cultural context, observer reliability, missing-data planning, attention to power in observer ratings, ethical review, and safeguards against misuse.
