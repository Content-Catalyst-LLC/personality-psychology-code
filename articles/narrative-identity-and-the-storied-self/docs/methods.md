# Methods Notes

This scaffold models narrative identity through linked dimensions:

1. redemption and contamination themes
2. narrative coherence
3. agency and communion
4. meaning-making
5. narrative flexibility
6. defensive rigidity
7. self-continuity
8. wellbeing
9. narrative growth orientation, narrative burden, and narrative integration
10. narrative-profile and constrained-pattern summaries

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated coding systems, coder training, intercoder reliability, careful sampling, trauma-informed protocols, consent, cultural context, attention to narrative power, and safeguards against misuse.
