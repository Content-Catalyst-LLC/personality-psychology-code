# Data Dictionary

Dataset: `synthetic_narrative_identity.csv`

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| `person_id` | Synthetic person identifier |
| `narrative_context` | Synthetic context shaping narrative identity and meaning-making |
| `redemption` | Degree to which difficult events are narrated as leading toward growth, purpose, or repair |
| `contamination` | Degree to which positive events are narrated as spoiled, reversed, or recoded as loss |
| `coherence` | Temporal, causal, and thematic organization of the life story |
| `agency` | Degree to which the narrator appears as an actor, chooser, witness, or responsible participant |
| `communion` | Relational, belonging, care, solidarity, and connection themes |
| `meaning_making` | Degree to which events are interpreted as significant for self-understanding |
| `narrative_flexibility` | Openness of the story to complexity, revision, and new evidence |
| `defensive_rigidity` | Rigidity, closure, denial, or over-neat interpretation in the story |
| `self_continuity` | Felt continuity across past, present, and future selves |
| `well_being` | Synthetic wellbeing indicator |
| `narrative_growth_orientation` | Composite of redemption, meaning-making, and agency |
| `narrative_burden` | Composite of contamination and defensive rigidity |
| `narrative_integration` | Composite of coherence, narrative flexibility, and self-continuity |
| `redemptive_agency_balance` | Redemption plus agency minus contamination and defensive rigidity |

The dataset is synthetic and not representative of actual people, survivors, patients, families, communities, cultures, institutions, legal subjects, or historical groups.
