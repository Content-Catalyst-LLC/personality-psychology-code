# Methods Notes

This scaffold models categorical and dimensional personality representation through linked dimensions:

1. continuous trait scores
2. thresholded categorical summaries
3. coarse profile labels
4. synthetic person-centered cluster profiles
5. boundary proximity around thresholds
6. cluster-boundary margins
7. information-loss summaries
8. continuous-versus-categorical model comparison
9. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated instruments, defined intended use, sampling design, reliability and validity evidence, fairness analysis, measurement-invariance testing where relevant, missing-data planning, privacy safeguards, ethical review, and governance controls for any consequential use.
