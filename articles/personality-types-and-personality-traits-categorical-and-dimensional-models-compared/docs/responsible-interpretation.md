# Responsible Interpretation

## Purpose

This repository supports professional education, research prototyping, consulting support, organizational learning, and methodological demonstration around personality types, personality traits, categorical thresholds, dimensional measurement, and profile summaries.

## Data Notes

The dataset is synthetic. It exists so workflows can be inspected, replicated, and extended without relying on sensitive human-subject data. The patterns are constructed examples for testing model architecture, not claims about actual people, teams, organizations, schools, workplaces, cultures, or institutions.

## Interpretive Standards

A type label, cluster assignment, diagnostic category, or trait score is a model-based representation. It is not the person. Dimensional scores usually preserve more information than thresholded labels. Categories may be useful for communication, teaching, screening, or applied thresholds, but their status depends on purpose, evidence, and consequences.

Boundary cases require particular caution. People just above and just below a threshold should not be treated as fundamentally different unless strong evidence supports that distinction. Cluster labels should be interpreted as summaries of profile patterns, not natural kinds, unless supported by strong replication and validation.

## Use Boundary

This scaffold is appropriate for analytic, educational, methodological, reflective, and professional-development purposes. It is not a standalone system for hiring, promotion, termination, clinical assessment, diagnosis, educational placement, legal evaluation, relationship matching, or individual prediction.
