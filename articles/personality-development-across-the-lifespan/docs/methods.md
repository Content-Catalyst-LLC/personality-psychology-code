# Methods Notes

This scaffold models personality development across the lifespan through a synthetic longitudinal design with repeated measurements of:

1. person identifier
2. wave and age
3. life stage
4. cohort and cultural context
5. neuroticism, extraversion, conscientiousness, openness, and agreeableness
6. role investment
7. repeated state-practice frequency
8. perceived support

The workflow estimates wave summaries, life-stage summaries, person-level change summaries, rank-order stability, mean-level change, and simple longitudinal model coefficients.

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, prospective design, measurement-invariance checks, appropriate sampling, cohort representation, cross-cultural design care, attrition handling, missing-data planning, ethical review, and safeguards against misuse.
