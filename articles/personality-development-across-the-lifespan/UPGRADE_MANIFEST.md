## Research-grade goals

This scaffold separates rank-order stability, mean-level change, trait trajectories, cohort context, cultural context, role investment, repeated state practice, perceived support, individual change scores, and longitudinal growth summaries. It avoids clinical assessment, personality testing, coaching guidance, workplace screening, educational placement, individual prediction, and reduction of persons to trajectory scores.
