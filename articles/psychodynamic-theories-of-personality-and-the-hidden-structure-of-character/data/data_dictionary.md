# Data Dictionary

Dataset: `synthetic_psychodynamic_personality.csv`

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| `person_id` | Synthetic person identifier |
| `developmental_context` | Synthetic developmental / relational context label |
| `mature_defenses` | Use of more flexible defenses such as humor, sublimation, anticipation, and suppression |
| `neurotic_defenses` | Use of intermediate defenses such as intellectualization, displacement, repression, and reaction formation |
| `immature_defenses` | Use of more distorting defenses such as denial, projection, splitting, and acting out |
| `defensive_rigidity` | Inflexibility and repetitiveness of defensive functioning |
| `attachment_anxiety` | Fear of abandonment, rejection, relational loss, or insufficient availability |
| `attachment_avoidance` | Discomfort with dependency, closeness, vulnerability, or relational need |
| `self_cohesion` | Stability and continuity of self-experience |
| `relational_security` | Capacity to experience relationships as safe, mutual, and repairable |
| `reflective_functioning` | Capacity to reflect on mental states in self and others |
| `character_integration` | Synthetic index of integrated self-other functioning and character organization |
| `symptom_distress` | Synthetic distress indicator associated with hidden-structure risk |
| `defensive_maturity` | Mature defenses minus average neurotic, immature, and rigid defensive burden |
| `attachment_insecurity` | Mean of attachment anxiety and attachment avoidance |
| `self_relational_capacity` | Mean of self-cohesion, relational security, and reflective functioning |
| `hidden_structure_risk` | Mean of attachment insecurity, immature defenses, and defensive rigidity |

The dataset is synthetic and not representative of actual people, patients, therapists, diagnoses, clinical populations, families, treatment programs, or institutions.
