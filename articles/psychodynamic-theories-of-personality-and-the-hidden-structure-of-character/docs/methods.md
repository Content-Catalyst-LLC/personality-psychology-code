# Methods Notes

This scaffold models psychodynamic personality structure through linked dimensions:

1. mature, neurotic, and immature defensive style
2. defensive rigidity
3. attachment anxiety and attachment avoidance
4. self-cohesion
5. relational security
6. reflective functioning
7. character integration
8. symptom distress
9. defensive maturity, attachment insecurity, self-relational capacity, and hidden-structure risk

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, ethical review, careful sampling, transparent construct definitions, clinical safeguards, missing-data planning, cultural context, and safeguards against misuse.
