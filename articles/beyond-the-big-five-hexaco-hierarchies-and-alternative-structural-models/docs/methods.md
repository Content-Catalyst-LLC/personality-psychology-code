# Methods Notes

This scaffold models alternative personality structures through linked psychometric dimensions:

1. Big Five-style broad domains
2. HEXACO-style broad domains
3. Honesty-Humility facet proxies
4. Emotionality and Agreeableness repartitioning
5. item-level structural comparison
6. five-versus-six-factor approximation
7. domain-versus-expanded-model prediction
8. integrity-related outcome comparison
9. facet granularity and bandwidth-fidelity tradeoffs
10. structural comparison indices
11. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real psychometric study would require validated instruments, factor-analytic model checking, reliability evidence, construct-validity evidence, criterion-validity evidence, measurement-invariance testing, missing-data analysis, sampling review, fairness analysis, privacy safeguards, and appropriate ethical/legal review for any applied or consequential use.
