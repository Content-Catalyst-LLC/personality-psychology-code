# Methods Notes

This scaffold models selfhood, agency, and personal identity through linked dimensions:

1. past, present, and future self-representation
2. temporal self-continuity
3. intentional clarity
4. action ownership
5. self-efficacy
6. external constraint
7. social recognition
8. value–commitment gap
9. identity alignment
10. identity integration and wellbeing
11. identity-profile and disjunction-pattern summaries

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, careful construct definition, ethical review, cultural and developmental context, safeguards for vulnerable populations, attention to coercion and recognition, and safeguards against misuse.
