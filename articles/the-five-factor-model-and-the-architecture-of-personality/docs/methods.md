# Methods Notes

This scaffold models Five-Factor architecture through linked psychometric dimensions:

1. broad domain scores
2. item-level responses
3. conscientiousness item examples
4. orderliness and industriousness facet examples
5. reliability by measurement level
6. domain-versus-facet prediction
7. broad life-functioning outcome comparison
8. hierarchy consistency and domain-facet alignment
9. bandwidth-fidelity comparison
10. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real psychometric study would require validated instruments, factor-analytic model checking, reliability evidence, construct-validity evidence, criterion-validity evidence, measurement-invariance testing, missing-data analysis, sampling review, fairness analysis, privacy safeguards, and appropriate ethical/legal review for any applied or consequential use.
