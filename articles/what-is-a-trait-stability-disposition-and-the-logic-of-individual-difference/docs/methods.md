# Methods Notes

This scaffold models trait reasoning through linked concepts:

1. multi-item trait scoring
2. internal consistency and reliability
3. repeated-state observations
4. person-level state aggregation
5. trait-state correlation
6. state variability
7. between-person and within-person variance
8. person-situation sensitivity
9. density-distribution logic
10. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated instruments, experience-sampling protocol review, missing-data analysis, measurement-invariance testing, sampling review, ethical review, privacy safeguards, psychometric validation, and careful governance for any applied or consequential use.
