# Responsible Interpretation

## Purpose

This repository supports professional education, research prototyping, consulting support, organizational learning, and methodological demonstration around the person–situation debate, behavioral consistency, repeated-measures design, conditional signatures, and dynamic trait expression.

## Data Notes

The dataset is synthetic. It exists so workflows can be inspected, replicated, and extended without relying on sensitive human-subject data. The patterns are constructed examples for testing model architecture, not claims about actual people, clients, students, workers, teams, organizations, schools, workplaces, cultures, or institutions.

## Interpretive Standards

A single behavior is not a complete personality revelation. A trait score is not a context-free behavioral guarantee. A situation is not a neutral backdrop. Personality interpretation should consider the person, the behavior, the situation, and the organized relation among them.

Apparent inconsistency can reflect adaptive responsiveness, role demand, power, trust, surveillance, discrimination, threat, autonomy, fatigue, care, or institutional constraint. Broad personality claims require repeated observations, relevant situation sampling, and proportional interpretation.

## Use Boundary

This scaffold is appropriate for analytic, educational, methodological, reflective, and professional-development purposes. It is not a standalone system for hiring, promotion, termination, clinical assessment, diagnosis, educational placement, legal evaluation, relationship matching, surveillance, or individual prediction.
