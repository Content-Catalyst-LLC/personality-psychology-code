# Methods Notes

This scaffold models the person–situation debate through linked dimensions:

1. broad person-level trait standing
2. momentary state expression
3. repeated occasions nested within persons
4. situational demand, sociality, evaluation, trust, autonomy, and threat
5. aggregation across occasions
6. within-person variability
7. person–situation interactions
8. conditional if–then signatures
9. density-distribution summaries
10. state inertia and lagged state patterns
11. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, sampling protocol design, missing-data planning, participant-burden review, informed consent, privacy safeguards, time-scale justification, reliability and validity evidence, fairness analysis, measurement-invariance testing where relevant, and governance controls for any consequential use.
