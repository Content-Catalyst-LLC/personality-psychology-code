# Data Provenance

The dataset in this directory is synthetic and generated by the upgrade script. It is not derived from relationship records, therapy records, survey respondents, private messages, families, friendship networks, clinical data, social media data, or any real community.

## Why synthetic data?

Synthetic data allow researchers, students, and readers to reproduce the code examples without using sensitive relational information or implying claims about actual individuals, partners, families, or communities.

## What the data can support

- Code demonstration
- Reproducibility testing
- Workflow validation
- Conceptual examples of relational-personality modeling
- Demonstrations of relationship satisfaction, social functioning, loneliness, support, and conflict analysis

## What the data cannot support

- Claims about real people or relationships
- Relationship diagnosis
- Clinical assessment
- Social ranking
- Couple or family evaluation
- Friendship scoring
- Claims about actual communities or relational systems
