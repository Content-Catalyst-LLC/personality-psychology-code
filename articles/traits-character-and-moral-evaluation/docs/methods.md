# Methods Notes

This scaffold models the relationship between descriptive traits and moral character evaluation through seven linked dimensions:

1. descriptive traits: Honesty-Humility, conscientiousness, agreeableness, emotional stability, openness
2. moral identity
3. practical judgment
4. institutional accountability
5. power pressure and social desirability pressure
6. moral-character-relevant outcomes: ethical behavior, integrity rating, trustworthiness rating
7. trait–character gap logic

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated instruments, ethical review, appropriate sampling, careful construct definition, cultural context, and strong safeguards against misuse.
