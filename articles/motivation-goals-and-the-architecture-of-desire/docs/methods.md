# Methods Notes

This scaffold models motivation, goals, and the architecture of desire through linked dimensions:

1. autonomy, achievement, belonging, security, meaning, and status goals
2. psychological need support: autonomy, competence, and relatedness
3. goal ownership and motivational quality
4. goal conflict and goal hierarchy
5. conscientiousness and persistence
6. adaptive disengagement
7. wellbeing
8. life-direction coherence
9. motivational profile groups and vulnerable patterns

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, careful sampling, cultural adaptation, measurement-invariance testing, longitudinal design where possible, missing-data planning, ethical review, and safeguards against misuse.
