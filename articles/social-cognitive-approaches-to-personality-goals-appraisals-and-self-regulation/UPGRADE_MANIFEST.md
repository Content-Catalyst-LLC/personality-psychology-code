## Research-grade goals

This scaffold separates person-level process tendencies, situation types, goal activation, appraisals, self-efficacy, self-regulation, perceived support, emotional arousal, prosocial behavior, avoidance behavior, task persistence, and if–then pattern summaries. It avoids personality testing, clinical assessment, educational placement, workplace screening, risk scoring, and reduction of persons to process scores.
