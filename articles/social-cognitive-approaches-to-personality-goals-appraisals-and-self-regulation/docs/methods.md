# Methods Notes

This scaffold models social-cognitive personality processes through repeated measurements of:

1. situation type
2. goal activation
3. threat appraisal
4. challenge appraisal
5. self-efficacy
6. self-regulation
7. emotional arousal
8. perceived support
9. prosocial behavior, avoidance behavior, and task persistence

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, appropriate sampling, careful situation coding, repeated-measures design, ethical review, missing-data planning, and safeguards against misuse.
