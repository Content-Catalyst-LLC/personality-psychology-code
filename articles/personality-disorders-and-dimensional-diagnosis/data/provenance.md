# Data Provenance

The dataset in this directory is synthetic and generated by the upgrade script. It is not derived from clinical records, diagnostic interviews, therapy notes, patient surveys, electronic health records, insurance files, court records, crisis records, or any real population.

## Why synthetic data?

Synthetic data allow researchers, students, and readers to reproduce the code examples without using sensitive clinical information or implying claims about actual patients, clinicians, treatment programs, or health systems.

## What the data can support

- Code demonstration
- Reproducibility testing
- Workflow validation
- Conceptual examples of dimensional diagnosis
- Demonstrations of severity, trait-domain, functioning-impairment, and profile-analysis workflows

## What the data cannot support

- Diagnosis
- Treatment planning
- Risk assessment
- Clinical decision support
- Personality assessment
- Insurance or eligibility decisions
- Patient ranking
- Claims about actual disorder prevalence
- Claims about actual clinical populations
