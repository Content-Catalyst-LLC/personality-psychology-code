# Methods Notes

This scaffold models lexical trait structure through linked psychometric and linguistic dimensions:

1. lexical descriptor ratings
2. descriptor-cluster scores
3. covariance and correlation structure
4. dimensionality reduction
5. five-factor and six-factor approximation
6. descriptor-cluster reliability
7. lexical abundance versus structural centrality
8. criterion prediction
9. cross-language and cultural caution indices
10. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real psycholexical study would require validated descriptor selection, language-specific lexical review, translation/back-translation where relevant, expert linguistic review, sampling review, factor-analytic model checking, reliability evidence, construct-validity evidence, measurement-invariance testing, fairness analysis, privacy safeguards, and appropriate ethical/legal review for any applied or consequential use.
