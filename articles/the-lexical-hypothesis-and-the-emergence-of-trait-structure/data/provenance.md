# Data Provenance

The datasets are generated synthetically by this repository script. They are not derived from personality inventories, lexical studies, clinical records, employment assessments, school records, human-subject research, app data, survey panels, psychometric vendors, organizational data, linguistic corpora, legal data, or any real population.

Synthetic data are used to make the workflow reproducible while avoiding sensitive human-subject data and avoiding claims about actual people, languages, workplaces, schools, communities, cultures, or institutions.
