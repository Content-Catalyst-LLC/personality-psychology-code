# Responsible Research Use

This repository is for reproducible, educational, and research-oriented analysis of maladaptive personality as a dimensional boundary problem.

It is appropriate for:

- code demonstration
- synthetic-data testing
- methodological prototyping
- article companion analysis
- transparent discussion of dimensional model limits

It is not appropriate for:

- diagnosis
- treatment planning
- risk assessment
- clinical decision support
- hiring or workplace screening
- custody decisions
- insurance scoring
- ranking people
- claims about actual people or clinical groups

Interpretation should preserve dignity. Personality-disorder language should not be used casually, pejoratively, or as a substitute for qualified clinical assessment. Apparent severity or impairment may reflect trauma, disability, neurodivergence, mood conditions, substance use, poverty, exclusion, culture, clinician bias, treatment access, or measurement design.
