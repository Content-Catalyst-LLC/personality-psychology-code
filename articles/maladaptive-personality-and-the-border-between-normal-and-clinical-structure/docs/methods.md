# Methods Notes

This scaffold models the border between normal personality variation and maladaptive clinical structure through seven linked dimensions:

1. maladaptive trait domains
2. self-functioning impairment
3. interpersonal-functioning impairment
4. rigidity
5. pervasiveness
6. contextual stress and support
7. clinical severity / liability / threshold-zone logic

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, qualified clinical assessment, appropriate sampling, cultural formulation, differential diagnosis, and ethical governance of sensitive data.
