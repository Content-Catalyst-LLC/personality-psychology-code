# Methods Notes

This scaffold models personality psychology through linked methodological dimensions:

1. broad personality item pools
2. trait-item reliability
3. trait score construction
4. dimensionality inspection
5. identity-linked outcome modeling
6. wellbeing and social functioning models
7. person-situation observations
8. person-situation interaction
9. measurement interpretation
10. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real personality psychology study would require validated instruments, sampling review, missing-data analysis, measurement-invariance testing, cultural and linguistic review, privacy safeguards, psychometric validation, and careful governance for any applied or consequential use.
