# Methods Notes

This scaffold models personality stability, intervention, and plasticity through a synthetic longitudinal design with repeated measurements of:

1. person identifier
2. wave / time
3. intervention or comparison group
4. age
5. neuroticism, extraversion, conscientiousness, openness, and agreeableness
6. role investment
7. repeated state-practice frequency
8. perceived support

The workflow estimates group-level wave summaries, person-level change summaries, rank-order stability, and simple longitudinal model coefficients.

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated measures, prospective design, appropriate sampling, attrition handling, preregistration where feasible, measurement-invariance checks, follow-up intervals, ethical review, and safeguards against misuse.
