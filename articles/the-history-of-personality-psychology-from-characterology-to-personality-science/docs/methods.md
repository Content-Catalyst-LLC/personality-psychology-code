# Methods Notes

This scaffold models the history of personality psychology through linked methodological dimensions:

1. typology versus dimensional scoring
2. broad item pools and latent structure
3. reliability and internal consistency
4. three-, five-, and six-factor comparisons
5. rank-order stability across two time points
6. person-situation interaction
7. measurement-invariance cautions
8. narrative and biographical integration
9. historical-method maturity
10. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real psychometric or historical personality study would require validated instruments, sampling review, archival/source criticism where relevant, measurement-invariance testing, cultural and linguistic review, privacy safeguards, and appropriate ethical/legal review for any applied or consequential use.
