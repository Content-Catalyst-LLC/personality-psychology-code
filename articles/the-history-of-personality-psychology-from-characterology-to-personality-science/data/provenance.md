# Data Provenance

The datasets are generated synthetically by this repository script. They are not derived from personality inventories, archival historical records, clinical records, employment assessments, school records, human-subject research, survey panels, psychometric vendors, organizational data, legal data, or any real population.

Synthetic data are used to make the workflow reproducible while avoiding sensitive human-subject data and avoiding claims about actual people, workplaces, schools, cultures, languages, communities, institutions, or historical groups.
