# Data Dictionary

Dataset: `synthetic_dark_traits_virtue_personality.csv`

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| `participant_id` | Synthetic participant ID |
| `institutional_context` | Synthetic social or institutional setting |
| `machiavellianism` | Strategic manipulation, cynicism, instrumental social calculation |
| `narcissism` | Entitlement, grandiosity, admiration seeking, status sensitivity |
| `psychopathy` | Callousness, low remorse, impulsive aggression, shallow affect |
| `sadism` | Interest in or gratification from others’ suffering or humiliation |
| `honesty_humility` | Sincerity, fairness, modesty, and resistance to greed or exploitation |
| `conscientious_reliability` | Moral reliability, follow-through, restraint, and responsibility |
| `fairness_orientation` | Tendency toward reciprocity, justice, and non-exploitative conduct |
| `compassion_kindness` | Responsiveness to suffering and concern for the welfare of others |
| `moral_identity` | Centrality of moral values to self-understanding |
| `practical_judgment` | Ability to apply moral understanding to concrete situations |
| `institutional_accountability` | Synthetic accountability, transparency, and constraint in the setting |
| `status_reward_pressure` | Synthetic pressure rewarding image, dominance, or ruthless advantage |
| `unethical_behavior` | Synthetic unethical conduct tendency |
| `prosocial_restraint` | Synthetic restraint from harm and willingness to act responsibly |
| `harm_indicator` | Synthetic harm-relevant outcome indicator |
| `dark_trait_burden` | Mean of Machiavellianism, narcissism, psychopathy, and sadism |
| `virtue_relevant_tendency` | Mean of Honesty-Humility, reliability, fairness, and compassion |
| `moral_integration_index` | Composite of virtue tendency, moral identity, and practical judgment |
| `dark_accountability_risk` | Dark burden multiplied by low institutional accountability |

The dataset is synthetic and not representative of any actual person, organization, leader, community, or institution.
