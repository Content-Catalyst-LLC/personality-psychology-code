# Methods Notes

This scaffold models moral personality structure through seven linked dimensions:

1. dark traits: Machiavellianism, narcissism, psychopathy, sadism
2. virtue-relevant tendencies: Honesty-Humility, reliability, fairness, compassion
3. moral identity
4. practical judgment
5. institutional accountability
6. status reward pressure
7. moral outcomes: unethical behavior, prosocial restraint, and harm indicators

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated instruments, ethical review, appropriate sampling, careful construct definition, cultural context, and strong safeguards against misuse.
