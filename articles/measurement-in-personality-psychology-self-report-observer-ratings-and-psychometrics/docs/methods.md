# Methods Notes

This scaffold models personality measurement through linked dimensions:

1. self-report item responses
2. observer-report item responses
3. item-level missingness
4. attention-check and response-quality flags
5. self-report and observer-report scale scores
6. internal consistency
7. self–other agreement
8. self–other discrepancy
9. method-effect indexing
10. professional-use boundaries and responsible interpretation

The workflow is designed for transparent method demonstration. A real study would require validated instruments, defined intended use, sampling design, reliability and validity evidence, factor-structure evaluation, response-process evidence, fairness analysis, measurement-invariance testing where relevant, missing-data planning, privacy safeguards, ethical review, and governance controls for any consequential use.
