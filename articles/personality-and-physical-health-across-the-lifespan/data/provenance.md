# Data Provenance

The dataset in this directory is synthetic and generated by the upgrade script. It is not derived from medical records, insurance files, patient surveys, clinical notes, wearable devices, public-health datasets, genomic data, mortality records, or any real population.

## Why synthetic data?

Synthetic data allow researchers, students, and readers to reproduce the code examples without using sensitive health information or implying claims about actual people, patients, communities, or health systems.

## What the data can support

- Code demonstration
- Reproducibility testing
- Workflow validation
- Conceptual examples of personality-health modeling
- Demonstrations of health behavior, stress burden, adherence, physical health, and functional ability analysis

## What the data cannot support

- Clinical prediction
- Diagnosis
- Treatment decisions
- Insurance or eligibility decisions
- Population ranking
- Claims about actual disease risk
- Claims about actual mortality risk
- Claims about real patients, communities, or health systems
